Delete Your Account

App: Stock Analyzer AI  |  Developer: Stock Analyzer AI

You can permanently delete your Stock Analyzer AI account and all associated data directly from within the app. Follow the steps below.

How to Delete Your Account

Steps to delete your account in the app:
  1. Open the Stock Analyzer AI app on your device.
  2. Tap the Profile icon in the top-right corner of the home screen.
  3. Go to Settings.
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
Warning: This action is permanent.

Once deleted, your account and all associated data cannot be recovered.

What Data Is Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data is permanently removed:

  1. Your account credentials and profile information (name, email address)
  2. Your watchlist and saved stocks
  3. Your remaining credit balance
  4. Your report history and any generated research reports
  5. All app activity and usage data associated with your account

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for a limited period after deletion as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., transaction records for tax and accounting compliance). This data is not used for any other purpose and is deleted as soon as the retention period expires.

Subscription and purchase records may be retained by third-party services (Google Play, Apple App Store, RevenueCat) in accordance with their own retention policies.
